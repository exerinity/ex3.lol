sparkmusic

An advanced music bot supporting pretty much every site on the internet. Started May 2023, shut down May 2024.
It started off being able to search from incompetech.com, Kevin MacLeod's music. I was pretty happy with that, as I never saw another bot do this. It eventually grew to over 1000 sites being supported.

sparkbot

Text-based command version of ex3, basically. Dates unknown, as I often started new builds at random times and shut them down.

sparkfirebot

My debut bot, it had over 200 commands. I started it in 2020 and shut it down June 2022, due to the difficulty of maintaining such a large bot. It also used discord.js v12, which had many problems in 2022.

Modango

A moderation-utility bot that was fairly simple and easy to maintain. I stopped working on it in January, but kept it online until August, "frozen in time". The name was a portmanteau of "moderation" and "mango". I started it some time in 2022, after closing sparkfirebot.

Days left Mastodon bot

A mastodon bot that posted how many days were left of the year, every day. It was extremely simple and light, using not even 2 MB of RAM. It only lasted until the end of 2023, and never saw 2024.

hastebot

A bot with the entire point of posting to hastebin. Finding it entirely useless and actually more complicated, it didn't even last 3 months. Made in 2022. Furthermore, Toptal changing everything about hastebin just poured more fuel onto the fire.
