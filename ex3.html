Privacy policy

(in a nutshell, full version soon)

The bot doesn't collect any data about you. The only data it collects is whenever you execute a command, an increment is added to how many total commands have been executed.
The data looks like this: 

400
Just a number. That's all it is.
And here's a basic demonstration on how it works: 
    let init = loadcommands();
    init += 1;
    save(init);
This occurs when ANYONE executes a command. It is the total count for everyone.

However, the APIs I use in commands may have a different privacy policy. You should see /credits and consult the proper site's privacy policy. And finally, you shouldn't enter any sensitive info into it, regardless of how private it is.
